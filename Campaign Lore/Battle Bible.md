## **🔮 Next Battle Setup: The Crystal Siege**

**Context:** The Slaves to Darkness have formed a desperate defensive perimeter around the **Spire of Reflections**. The Drained Chaos Sorcerer has tethered his remaining life force to four ancient **Fractal Nodes** scattered across the crystalline plains, attempting to draw enough ambient magic to mend his broken army. The Gloomspite Gitz, sensing weakness and drawn by the shiny glass, are swarming the perimeter to shatter the ritual.

**Special Rules:**

1. **The Fractal Nodes (Asymmetrical Objectives):** Place 4 Objective markers outside the deployment zones. These are the Fractal Nodes.  
   * *Chaos Ritual:* Any Slaves to Darkness Hero within 3" of a Node at the end of their turn can channel it. Choose one friendly unit anywhere on the battlefield to immediately heal D3 Wounds.  
   * *Gitz Vandalism:* Any Gloomspite Gitz unit that controls a Node at the end of their turn can smash it. The Node is removed from play.  
2. **The Spire's Pulse (Global Effect):** At the start of the 3rd, 4th, and 5th Battle Rounds, the Spire unleashes a shockwave of localized reality distortion.  
   * *Controlled Pulse:* If 3 or more Nodes are still on the battlefield, the pulse is stable. All Slaves to Darkness units add 1 to their Move characteristic for the round, and all Gitz units must subtract 1 from Run and Charge rolls.  
   * *Wild Backlash:* If 2 or fewer Nodes remain, the magic fractures. Every unit on the battlefield (Friend or Foe) rolls a D6. On a 1-2, that unit suffers D3 Mortal Damage as glass shards erupt from the ground.  
3. **The Tethered Sorcerer:** The Chaos Sorcerer Lord begins the battle deployed in the exact center of the board, tethered to the Nexus Chaotica. He cannot Move, Run, or Charge, but he projects a 12" aura granting a **Ward (5+)** to all friendly Slaves to Darkness units.

**Victory Condition:**

* **Gloomspite Gitz (Major Victory):** Assassinate the tethered Chaos Sorcerer Lord AND destroy the Nexus Chaotica.  
* **Slaves to Darkness (Major Victory):** Survive until the end of Battle Round 5 with at least 2 Fractal Nodes still intact.
