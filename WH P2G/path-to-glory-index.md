## P2G Aftermath.md

Aftermath sequence — performed in this order after each battle:

| Step | Action | Key Details |
|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Earn Glory Points** | Based on battle size, participation, victory type, general survival |
| 2 | **Gain Renown Points** | Survivors: D3 renown; Casualties: 1 renown; Favoured Warrior: D6 renown |
| | *Update Ranks & Paths* | Aspiring (5–14 renown) → choose a Path; new rank → choose 1 of 2 abilities |
| 3 | **Complete Quests** | Check progress, claim rewards, abandon, or pick new quests |
| 4 | **Manage Order of Battle** | Spend Glory Points (only 1 of Actions A/B per aftermath) |
| | *Action A: Add New Unit* | Cost = unit point cost; max 1 new unit |
| | *Action B: Reinforce Unit* | Cost = unit point cost; max 1 reinforcement |
| | *Action C: Retire Units* | Free; loses all renown & abilities |

## P2G Quests.md

Quests available for selection (or AI assignment):

| Quest | Objective | Progress | Completion | Reward |
|-------|-----------|----------|------------|--------|
| **Search for the Artefact** | Find a relic | HERO near terrain, not in combat, not in friendly territory → 4+: 1 quest point | 3+ quest points | 1 artefact of power to an eligible HERO |
| **Master Magical Lore** | Master a spell | Casting roll of 8+ → 1 quest point | 3+ quest points | 1 spell from faction spell lore |
| **Learn Ancient Scriptures** | Decipher archaic texts | PRIEST given 4+ ritual points → 1 quest point | 5+ quest points | 1 prayer from faction prayer lore |
| **Harness Manifestation** | Tame a wild manifestation | Pick a Wild spell/prayer; roll each use: 1–3 = mortal damage, 4+ = quest points equal to roll | 10+ quest points | Spell/prayer loses Wild status |
| **Seek Glory in Battle** | Prove warriors in battle | Pick a unit as Glory Seekers | Win major/minor victory; Glory Seekers participated & not destroyed | D3+3 additional renown |
| **Rise of a Champion** | Slay a powerful foe | Pick a HERO without a heroic trait as Rising Champion | Enemy HERO or MONSTER slain by Rising Champion | 1 heroic trait for Rising Champion |
