According to the provided Path to Glory rules, here are all the actions you can take during the Aftermath Sequence, performed in this specific order1:

### **1\. Earn Glory Points**

* **Calculate Earnings:** Tally points based on the size of the battle and your achievements (Participation, Victory type, General survival)2.  
* **Record:** Update the total Glory Points on your roster3.

### **2\. Gain Renown Points**

* **Award Points:**  
  * **Survivors:** Give **D3** renown to every unit that participated and was not destroyed4.  
  * **Casualties:** Give **1** renown to every unit that participated and was destroyed5.  
  * **Favoured Warriors:** Choose **1 non-Warlord unit** that participated to receive **D6** renown instead of the standard amount6.  
* **Update Ranks & Paths:**  
  * **Check Ranks:** Compare new totals to the Rank table (Aspiring: 5-14, Elite: 15-29, etc.)7.  
  * **Select Path:** If a unit reaches **Aspiring (5 renown)** for the first time, you must permanently choose a Path (e.g., Attacker, Defender, Warrior, Mage)8.  
  * **Choose Abilities:** If a unit achieves a new rank, choose **1 of the 2 abilities** listed for that rank on their Path9.

### **3\. Complete Quests**

* **Check Progress:** Verify if you met the completion conditions for your current quest during the battle10.  
* **Claim Reward:** If completed, gain the listed reward (e.g., artefact, spell, extra renown) and clear the quest from your log11.  
* **Give Up:** You may choose to abandon a current quest. It is removed, and you gain no reward12.  
* **Pick New Quest:** If your Quest Log is empty (because you completed or abandoned a quest), you may pick a new one immediately13.

### **4\. Manage Your Order of Battle**

You may spend your accumulated Glory Points (GP) here. **You are limited to performing only ONE of the first two actions below per aftermath sequence**14.

* **Action A: Add New Unit:** Spend GP equal to a unit's point cost to add it to your roster at its minimum unit size15151515.  
  * *Limit:* Max 1 new unit per aftermath16.  
* **Action B: Reinforce Unit:** Spend GP equal to a unit's point cost to increase an existing unit's size (reinforce it)17.  
  * *Limit:* Max 1 reinforcement per aftermath18.  
* **Action C: Retire Units:** You may permanently remove any number of units from your roster. This costs 0 GP, but all renown and abilities for that unit are lost19.
